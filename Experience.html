IT Analyst

Summer 2019

During the summer of 2019, I worked as an IT Analyst for the company PC Financial. The specific stores that the team I was a part of managed were the Mobile Shop kiosks located inside PFC stores like Loblaws. I spent my time there keeping records and organizing their hardware inventory, provisioning new/replacement hardware for stores, and remote troubleshooting of equipment. I gained experience in networking, and spent a lot of time working with routers and security cameras, determining which hardware was operational, and if they were not operational, instructing store workers over the phone on finding and fixing the hardware issues. I was also put in charge of provisioning equipment for stores. If a point of sale computer went down, I would take the ticket and setup a replacement device to send to the store. This involved writing and running batch scripts to set up the necessary software. During my time there, I was able to clear out their backlog of hardware supports tickets in their system.

Research Assistant

Summer 2021

The work I did over this summer position led into my capstone project, which was supervised by the same professor. This project had a lot to do with variable speed transmissions and induction machines. To learn more about the capstone project you can navigate to the "projects" tab at the top of the screen. I did a lot of experiments and tests with induction generators, alternators, and small engines. I also worked with the other research assistants on the development of the stirling engine itself. During this time I also repaired and maintained some of the lab equipment. Including the lab's 2 3D printers.
