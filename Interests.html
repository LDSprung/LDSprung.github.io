Interests

3D printing

As you may have seen from browsing the other pages on this site, 3D printing is a large hobby of mine. I have owned my own printer for a few years now, and it has been one of the best purchases I have made. I enjoy everything from designing and creating small updrages for my desk and my room, to larger prop making projects of things from movies and video games.

Space Exploration

I am interesting in anything to do with the space program. I have been a fan of science fiction all my life and it has fueled my desire to become an engineer. My dream job would be to work in the space program in some capacity, but until then, I spend some of my free time catching up and learning about the most recent advances in space technology.

Dissability Studies

One of my big interests is the blend between engineering and dissability studies. This started back when I first bought my 3D printer, I did a lot of research into 3D printed prosthetics, and I have been researching the topic in my spare time ever since. I think that using engineering to help peope with dissabilities is an amazing application of knowledge, and I am always interested in ways that I can apply my knowledge in this way.

Robotics

Robotics was a large part of my education, and where I drew the most interest during my time in university. I have studied robotics and automation, as well as mobile robotics, and I have become very attached to working in this field. I would love to be able to work on robotics as the technology advances further and further, and to be a part of the evolutionary leap in this branch of engineering and applied science.

Web Development

Web development has become a recent interest of mine. This very website was created out of a desire to learn more about it and to enhance my skillset. I feel as though I have only just scratched the surface of web development, but I am eager to learn more and grow my knowledge on this topic to be able to create bigger and better projects.

Aquatic Engineering

I have a particular interest in technology that is designed to be used underwater. I was a swimmer for many years, and have spent a lot of time underwater, and subsequently a lot of time wondering what kinds of technology could be used underwater in ways that it isnt currently.
